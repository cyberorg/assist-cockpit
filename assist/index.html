Warranty support channels


Every My sCool Server product has a sticker bearing a unique MSS
ID or S/N (Serial Number). This ID is important for all
communications with Recherche Tech in regards to your MSS hardware.
If you ever have an issue with your MSS hardware and it is under
warranty, simply open a ticket through any of the support channels
and quote this MSS ID and the nature of your problem.
